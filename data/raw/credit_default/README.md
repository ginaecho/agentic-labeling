# Default of Credit Card Clients (Humans / Financial)

**Source**: UCI ML Repository (id=350)
**Entity column**: `ID`
**Use case for this pipeline**: Cluster credit-card customers by payment history + credit usage; identify default-risk personas

## Description
30,000 credit card holders in Taiwan. 24 features: credit limit, sex, education, marital status, age, 6 months of payment status, 6 months of bill amounts, 6 months of payments, default label.
